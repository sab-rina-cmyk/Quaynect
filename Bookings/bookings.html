Policies

Loaning Periods

Most items can be borrowed for up to 7 days. High-demand equipment may have shorter loan periods.

Fees & Deposits

A refundable deposit may be required for certain high-value tools. Overdue items incur a small daily fee.

Care & Use

Please handle all equipment with care. Ensure items are cleaned and properly stored after use.

Returning Items

Items must be returned to the main office during operational hours. Do not leave items unattended.

Damaged Items

Report any damage immediately. Residents may be responsible for repair or replacement costs for negligence-based damage.

Space Booking Guide

Shared spaces can be booked up to 2 weeks in advance via the app. Please vacate the space promptly at the end of your session.

Equipment Maintenance

Regular maintenance is performed on all tools. If you notice any technical issues, please flag them within the app.

Frequently Asked Questions

Check our online portal for a full list of FAQs regarding equipment, spaces, and community guidelines.
